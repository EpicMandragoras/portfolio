Preventia: Empowering Allergy Sufferers

Preventia is an app designed to make life easier for people with allergies. By offering real-time allergen information, community-driven insights, and location tracking, it helps users navigate everyday situations with confidence and ease. Developed with empathy, Preventia aims to alleviate the daily struggles of managing allergies, empowering users to make better decisions about their health.

Preventia helps users identify allergens in food by inputting ingredients and checking for potential allergic reactions.

Users needed a fast, clear way to check food ingredients and allergens, making the interface user-friendly and informative.

The app utilizes clean fonts and bright colors.

The design highlights allergens with intuitive icons, immediate alerts for potential risks, and user-friendly features like weather and map alerts.

The app empowers users to easily track allergens in food and products, ensuring a safe and personalized experience. The website version extends the app's capabilities, offering additional data and user insights.
