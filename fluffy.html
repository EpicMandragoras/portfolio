Fluffypals

A user-friendly shopping app for linens and towels, offering personalized options and a smooth, efficient checkout experience. Designed with a clean layout and intuitive navigation, it makes shopping easy and enjoyable.

The goal was to create a seamless shopping experience for linens and towels, combining a friendly, approachable design with functional features like easy navigation and personalization.

The design focused on a clean layout, clear call-to-action buttons, and a simplified checkout process. Personalized options for items like color and size were also prioritized.

Users were often overwhelmed with the variety of options and struggled with slow checkouts. A solution was needed for intuitive filtering and an easy-to-use shopping cart system.

Various mock-ups show the design flow, from browsing towels to checking out. Font and color choices reinforce a fresh, approachable vibe.
The redesigned app enhances the user experience by streamlining interactions and creating a more engaging, efficient shopping environment.
